School Trips at Riget Zoo Adventures

Welcome to Riget Zoo Adventures, where learning comes alive! Our school trip packages offer an exciting opportunity for students to immerse themselves in the wonders of the natural world. With interactive exhibits, educational programs, and close encounters with wildlife, a visit to the zoo is sure to inspire curiosity and ignite a passion for conservation.

Why Choose Riget Zoo Adventures for Your School Trip?

Educational Value: Our zoo is committed to providing enriching educational experiences for students of all ages. Our exhibits are designed to align with curriculum standards and encourage hands-on learning.

Diverse Wildlife: From majestic lions to playful penguins, our zoo is home to a wide variety of species from around the globe. Students will have the opportunity to observe animals up close and learn about their habitats, behaviours, and conservation status.

Interactive Learning: Our interactive exhibits and educational programs offer students the chance to engage their senses and deepen their understanding of the natural world. From feeding demonstrations to behind-the-scenes tours, there’s always something new to discover at Riget Zoo Adventures.

Safety: The safety and well-being of our guests are our top priorities. Our experienced staff members are trained to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors, and we adhere to strict safety guidelines and protocols.

Convenience: Planning a school trip can be stressful, but we’re here to help every step of the way. Our dedicated education team will work with you to customize your itinerary, arrange transportation, and coordinate lunch options, making your trip to the zoo hassle-free.

Our School Trip Packages:

Standard Package: Includes admission to the zoo, guided tours, educational presentations, and access to all exhibits.

Enhanced Package: In addition to the standard offerings, the enhanced package includes exclusive behind-the-scenes tours, hands-on activities, and special animal encounters.

Customized Packages: Have specific educational objectives or interests? Let us know, and we’ll work with you to create a customized itinerary tailored to your group’s needs.
